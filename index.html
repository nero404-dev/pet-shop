Frequently Asked Questions
FAQs

How do I sell my car to your dealership?

Selling your car to us is simple! Just contact our manager to schedule an appointment. During your visit, we will evaluate your vehicle and provide you with a fair offer based on its condition and market value. If you accept the offer, we'll handle all the paperwork for you!

What information do I need to provide when selling my car?

When selling your car, please provide the vehicle title, registration, and any service records you have. Additionally, it's helpful to know the car's mileage and any previous accidents or repairs. This information will help us give you the best possible offer.

How long does the selling process take?

The selling process can typically be completed in just a few hours. After you bring your car in for evaluation, we will make you an offer. If you accept, we'll finalize the paperwork and handle the payment on the same day. However, the exact time may vary depending on the specific circumstances of your sale.
